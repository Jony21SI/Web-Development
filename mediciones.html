Unit Conversions
Temperature




To convert celsuis to fahrenheit yourself, use this formula replacing the `C` with your temperature in celsuis: (C × 9/5) + 32
Weight




To convert Kilograms to pounds yourself, use this formula replacing the `K` with your weight in kilograms: (kg × 2.205)
Distance




To convert Kilometers to Miles yourself, use this formula replacing the `Km` with your distance in kilometer: (km ÷ 1.609)
